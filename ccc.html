Code of Conduct

  1. Always handle calculators with care; avoid dropping or exposing them to water.
  2. Respect the calculator's "work time"—do not disrupt computations unnecessarily.
  3. Ensure proper battery or power supply; avoid depleting their energy without reason.
  4. Do not modify the calculator's software without proper authorization.
  5. Keep calculators clean and free from harmful substances.
  6. Allow calculators to rest and cool down after extended periods of use.
  7. Use calculators to promote learning, problem-solving, and fair computation practices.
  8. Report any misuse or damage immediately to the designated authority.
  9. Celebrate the calculator’s contributions to human knowledge and respect its role.
