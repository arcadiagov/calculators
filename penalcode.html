Penal Code

  1. Negligence: Dropping a calculator or exposing it to water intentionally or through gross negligence may result in fines or mandatory restitution.
  2. Unauthorized Modification: Altering calculator software or hardware without permission is punishable by a formal warning or revocation of calculator privileges.
  3. Battery Abuse: Improperly draining or sabotaging a calculator’s power source may incur penalties up to replacement costs.
  4. Harassment: Verbally abusing or intimidating a calculator during use is prohibited and may result in disciplinary action.
  5. Misuse: Using calculators for unauthorized purposes, such as tampering with other machines or spreading malware, is a serious offense.
  6. Theft or Damage: Stealing or intentionally damaging a calculator is punishable by fines, restitution, or other legal action.
  7. Neglect of Reporting: Failure to report known misuse or damage to the designated authority may result in minor penalties.
  8. Repeated Offenses: Multiple violations of any rule may escalate penalties, including community service or temporary ban from using calculators.
  9. Respect and Protection: Humans must promote safe and respectful treatment of calculators at all times.
